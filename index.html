About

Craft, curiosity,
and code

Vishal is a Principal iOS Engineer who loves building things from scratch. He has shipped production apps across fintech, health-tech, social media, and e-commerce -- each time designing architecture that scales and stays maintainable.

He's drawn to the "zero to one" phase of products: laying out module boundaries, automating tedious workflows with Swift scripts, and mentoring teams to move with confidence. Clean code and reusable components are non-negotiable.

Currently exploring the intersection of AI and mobile -- learning to orchestrate LLMs, agents, and AI tools to multiply productivity. The long-term goal is to ship products faster by blending engineering craft with AI-augmented workflows, always learning from others along the way.
