Privacy Policy

Tim-Bak-Toe (XO3) — Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

Tim-Bak-Toe ("XO3", "the app") is a tic-tac-toe game developed by an independent developer. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains what data is and isn't collected.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or any form of data collection. No account creation is required to play the game.

Game Center

The app integrates with Apple Game Center for online multiplayer functionality. When you use Game Center:

  • Authentication and matchmaking are handled entirely by Apple
  • Your Game Center display name is visible to opponents during online matches
  • We do not access, store, or transmit any Game Center profile data

Apple's handling of Game Center data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (sound settings, timer duration, appearance mode) locally on your device using standard system storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data. The only external service used is Apple Game Center, as described above.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
